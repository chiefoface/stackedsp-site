Our Belief

The best teams win

In the age of AI, team size is shrinking. A single exceptional engineer can outperform a department. This makes every hire more consequential than ever before. We exist to ensure you make the right ones.

What We Look For

Talent built for the future

Technical skill is table stakes. We search for something rarer—the traits that separate great engineers from transformative ones.

High Agency

People who don't wait for permission. They see what needs to be done and move. They own outcomes, not just tasks. In a startup, this is the difference between momentum and stagnation.

Relentless Curiosity

The best engineers are perpetually unsatisfied with surface-level understanding. They dig deeper, ask better questions, and find connections others miss. Curiosity compounds.

Fast Learners—and Unlearners

The landscape shifts constantly. What worked yesterday may not work tomorrow. We find people who absorb new paradigms quickly—and have the intellectual honesty to abandon what no longer serves.

Critical Thinkers

Not contrarians for the sake of it, but people who evaluate ideas on their merits. They stress-test assumptions, challenge weak reasoning, and make decisions grounded in clarity.
